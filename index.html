Privacy Policy - AquaGo

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to AquaGo, a smart drinking water delivery application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect and why
  • How your data is stored and protected
  • Your rights regarding personal data
  • How you can contact us

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or place orders using AquaGo, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (for OTP login and delivery contact)
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • User role (customer, vendor, delivery partner)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Device and browser information
  • App usage activity
  • Order history and timestamps
  • Error logs and diagnostics

1.3 Location Data

  • Customers: Used to identify delivery location
  • Delivery partners: Real-time location for order tracking (with permission)

1.4 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways. AquaGo does not store debit/credit card or UPI details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Process water can orders and subscriptions
  • Enable real-time delivery tracking
  • Send order confirmations and notifications
  • Improve app performance and service quality

3. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Secure authentication methods
  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular system updates

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and update your profile information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Delete your account
  • Opt out of promotional notifications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication & notifications)
  • Google Maps API (delivery location & tracking)
  • Payment gateways (secure payments)

All third-party services follow strict data protection standards.

6. Children's Privacy

AquaGo is not intended for children under the age of 13. If such data is identified, it will be removed immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be notified through the app or website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns, contact us:

  • Email: support@aquago.com
  • App: Use the "Contact Us" option inside the AquaGo app
